Considering not being able to go to Museums, looking at pictures virtually doesn't satisfy the same way it would in person.
I have been on some of those virtual tours, and don't get me wrong it's awesome that we can go for free, BUT I appreciate it more in person.
I am able to see the intricate lines and curves of the painting. This might be weird but you might think the same thing...that museum smell.
It's a weird thing I know, but I find smells also add something to an experience wiether we appreciate them or not. I also like the fact that in person I can admire the size of a big painting.

I remember in High School I would look for any reason to join a feild trip to the Chicago Art Institute. I find happiness being surrounded by,
not only the history, but artistic talent. I wish I was good enough at art to be in a muesum not because of recognition but the idea of that
a piece of me could forever be in my happy place is neat. Anyway, I also though this picture was cute! I saw it on the news, they had to make a
new mask because someone stole the first one. :(
