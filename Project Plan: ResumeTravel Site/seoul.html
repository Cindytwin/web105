Food Markets

Probably the most popular food markets you can find in Seoul is the Gwangjang Market
This traditional street market is the oldest and largest in South Korea. There you will see over 5,000 shops
and over 20,000 employees! Thats so mind blowing! Not really but it's impressive non the less.

Banpo Bridge

Banpo Bridge stands over the Han river, and connects Seocho and Yongsan districts.
This bridge is interesting because it is a double deck bridge. An interesting feature about this bridge
is that it has a water fountain installed into it's sides. It shoots out aproximetly 190 tons of water per minute!
The water comes from the river itself. At night you can catch a show of the Rainbow lights when the fountain turns on.
